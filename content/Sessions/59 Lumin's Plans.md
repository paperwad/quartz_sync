# Summary 
[[People/Lumin]] uses the [[heart of the shadow beast]] to talk to [[People/Varathos]]. [[People/Varathos]] explains that once they reverse the [[Concepts/Prophecy]] [[People/Lumin]] and [[People/Onora]] will no longer be important. [[People/Lumin]] swears to go to the island of [[Locations/Burge/Xendrik]] to meet physically with [[People/Varathos]]. In an emergency [[People/Lumin]] can use the [[heart of the shadow beast]] one last time to contact him. [[People/Lumin]] keeps this a secret from everyone and modifies [[People/Zareth]]'s memory into thinking that [[People/Vaeril Duskthorn]] gave him an order to go to [[Locations/Burge/Xendrik]]. It doesn't work.
