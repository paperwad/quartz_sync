## Healing Winds 
The Sylphwing spreads its wings, releasing a surge of life-giving wind. Up to 3 creatures of its choice within 30 feet regain 4d8+4 hit points and are cleansed of one disease or condition (blinded, deafened, or poisoned).
## Wind Walk
## Gale Barrier
When the Sylphwing or an ally within 10 feet is hit by a ranged attack, the Sylphwing can use its reaction to summon a burst of wind, imposing disadvantage on the attack roll.
## Cyclone Rush (Recharge 5-6)
The Sylphwing unleashes a powerful gust of wind in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 15 Strength saving throw, taking 22 (4d10) bludgeoning damage and being pushed 20 feet on a failed save, or half as much damage and not pushed on a successful one.
