## Soulbrand Spike
Type: Action (to implant) / Bonus Action (to trigger)  
Range: 60 feet  
Target: One creature  
Duration: Until triggered or removed  
Recharge: Usable once per short rest (or scale this based on balance needs)  
  
Implant Phase (Action):  
You launch a spectral spike of shadowed necrotic energy that embeds itself subtly into a creature’s body. The target must succeed on a Perception check (DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus + your spellcasting modifier) upon being hit to notice the implant.  
  
If Perception check succeeds: The creature is aware of the implant and can take actions to try to remove it (e.g., Remove Curse, Greater Restoration, etc.) or simply anticipate the effect.  
If Perception check fails: The implant remains unnoticed and hidden within.  
(The spike cannot be removed by mundane means and is nearly undetectable by magical identification unless specifically looking for soulbound magic.)  
  
Trigger Phase (Reaction Between Turns):  
You may use a Reaction to attempt to trigger the implant.  
  
If the creature is above 10% of its maximum hit points, the trigger fizzles harmlessly.  
If the creature is at or below 10% of its maximum hit points, the soulbound energy lashes out from within.  
Attack Roll:  
  
If the creature noticed the implant, make a spell attack roll (flat).  
If the creature did not notice the implant, make a spell attack roll with advantage.  
On Hit:  
The target takes 3d10 + your spellcasting modifier necrotic damage at levels 5–10.  
Increase to 5d10 + modifier at level 11–16, and 7d10 + modifier at level 17+.  
  
On Miss: The energy is expelled harmlessly.
## Death's Touch
Raise undead creatures up to 10 ft. away and up to the maximum amount available. All creatures must be lower level (Weaker) than yourself. Must raise within 1 minute of their death. The Summons cannot be more than 100 ft. away from the summoner, or they lose all connection and die.
