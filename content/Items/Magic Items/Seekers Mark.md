**Marked Precision (3 Charges):**

- The crossbow holds **3 charges** that replenish after a long rest.
- When the wielder makes an attack roll with the crossbow, they can choose to expend 1 charge to add a **+2 bonus to the attack roll** and deal an additional **1d6 force damage** on a hit.

---

**Seeking Bolt:**

- If the wielder misses an attack, they can expend 2 charges to reroll the attack roll. The wielder must use the new roll.

---

**Runic Activation:**

- As a bonus action, the wielder can expend 1 charge to cast the _Hunter's Mark_ spell (no concentration required, lasts for 1 minute) on a target they can see.
