**Wondrous Item, Very Rare (requires attunement by a spellcaster or monk)**

A tight-wound necklace of bone and shimmering metal plates, etched with hundreds of micro-script runes. When activated (bonus action), it unfolds like a blooming scroll and wraps the wearer in flowing **armor of parchment and scripture**, which constantly shifts and reconfigures like drifting ink.

#### ✨ **While Worn:**

- **Once Per Short Rest AC** becomes 17 if unarmored for 5 Minutes.
    
- You gain resistance to **psychic** and **force** damage.
    
- When you cast a spell of 1st level or higher, you can cause one target to **automatically fail** a concentration check on a hit or fail to counterspell your magic (1/day).

-  As a reaction, you may rewrite a magical effect you see cast within 30 ft. This lets you change the _target_ of a single-target spell as it’s cast (once per long rest).
    

🌀 _The armor rustles softly with whispers only the wearer can hear, and the script changes when they speak, echoing their intent._
