1d4 piercing + 2d8 radiant 

For one minute, the dagger radiates divine light and gains the following effects:  
  
Attacks with the dagger deal an additional 2d8 radiant damage.  
If an ally is reduced to 0 HP within 30 feet, the wielder can use their reaction to transfer half the radiant damage dealt (rounded down) to heal that ally instead.
