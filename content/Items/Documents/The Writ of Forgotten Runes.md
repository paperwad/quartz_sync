### Mist rune ✅
**Description:** Bound in dark green leather with faint golden runes along the spine. The book’s pages are brittle and incomplete, containing fragments of forgotten arcane knowledge.

- **Incomplete Spell 1: Glyph of Binding Light (Level 1 Abjuration)**

- _This spell is half-complete, missing a key component to finalize the incantation._
- **Effect:** Glyph of light that anchors a creature within a 15-foot radius. They must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be bound for 1 minute. The rest of the incantation is faded.
- **Incomplete Spell 2: Veil of Mist (Level 2 Conjuration)**

- **Effect:** The caster creates a thin mist that masks their presence, giving advantage on Stealth checks for 1 minute. The instructions end abruptly after describing the mist’s form, hinting at more advanced uses of this spell that remain lost.
