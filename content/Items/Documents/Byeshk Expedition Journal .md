 **once wielded magic with pride, a Sorcerer of the Onyx Guild, feared and revered. The Abyssal Order changed all that, branding the unsanctioned practice of magic as treason. When the purge came, I fled, abandoning the halls I had once defended with my life. The Byeshk Mountains became my refuge, their rugged peaks and winding trails a labyrinth to confound even the most determined pursuers.

Year 1 of the Byeshk Expedition  
The mountains are as enigmatic as they are treacherous. Rivers twist through valleys, their waters sparkling like starlight. Cliffs rise like jagged teeth to pierce the heavens. I have begun mapping these lands, detailing their majesty and secrets. Each night, by the light of a conjured flame, I trace the day’s travels onto parchment. A poor substitute for my lost guild, but it gives me purpose.

Year 3 of the Byeshk Expedition  
Strange noises echo through the peaks—low rumbles that make the earth tremble. Trolls, I suspect, though I have yet to see them. My provisions run low, yet the thought of descending into the lowlands terrifies me. Rumors of the Abyssal Order’s shadow looming ever closer haunt me.

Year 5 of the Byeshk Expedition  
This cave... it called to me. The air within is damp, the stone walls carved with ancient markings I cannot decipher. I made camp here, though an unease gnaws at the edges of my thoughts. My dreams are vivid, filled with shadows and the whispers of long-dead voices.
