 **Clue in the Spellbook:** Another incomplete page reveals fragments of **ancient runes**, cracked and partially eroded by time. A small note in the margin says: _“Rune lost to time... the crystal holds the key."  

 The party hears tales of an **abandoned dwarven temple** hidden beneath the [[Locations/Khorvaire/Shadowcrags]], where ancient rune-forging took place. Legends say the dwarves used magical **Crystal Runestones** to engrave powerful runes into their weapons and armor, but the temple was abandoned after a catastrophic event.
