Clue in the Spellbook: One of the pages seems to be drawn with an inky, shadow-like substance that shifts when touched. The page has faded, but there’s an eerie whisper written at the bottom: “In shadows you’ll find the ink of truth.”

Adventure:
The party learns of a cursed Shadow Beast that roams the forest at night, stalking travelers and feeding off their fear. Legend says the creature’s blood can be used as Ink of Shadows, a rare material needed to complete certain spells, especially those related to illusion magic.

Beast is on the other side of [[Locations/Khorvaire/Lake Galifar]] according to [[General Oak]]
